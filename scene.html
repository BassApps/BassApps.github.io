Maschine Scene Selector

Maschine Scene Selector lets you switch scenes without having to press the "Scene" button on your Maschine. This can make your life easier as it helps a lot during live performances.

Features

  • A couple of scene buttons, haven't counted them
  • A 1-octave keyboard (not seen in the video)
  • Dedicated button to jump to the next scene
  • Works with Maschine, Maschine MK2 and Maschine Studio
